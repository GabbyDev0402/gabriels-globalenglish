Meet Gabriel - Your Partner in English Learning

Hi, I'm Gabriel! I'm a TEFL-certified ESL tutor passionate about helping students like you achieve fluency and confidence in English.

My Teaching Journey

My passion for languages and connecting with people from different cultures led me to ESL tutoring. I've always enjoyed helping others learn and grow, and I find immense satisfaction in seeing my students achieve their language goals. My experience includes teaching at Washington Christian School. I am committed to creating personalized lessons that work for each individual student. Previously, as a sales manager for Algorithmics, I honed skills relevant to teaching and tutoring such as planning and client interaction, which enables me to better adapt to the needs of my students, while being able to provide a learning plan and tutoring service that fits their goals.

My Teaching Approach

I believe that learning English should be fun, engaging, and relevant to your life. My teaching approach focuses on practical communication skills, using interactive activities, real-world examples, and multimedia resources. I create a supportive and encouraging learning environment where you feel comfortable asking questions and making mistakes. I tailor lessons to each student's needs and learning style, ensuring you get the personalized attention you deserve.

My Skills and Expertise

I am proficient in teaching the following areas for students across various levels:

  • Grammar
  • Vocabulary
  • Reading Comprehension
  • Conversational Practice

What Makes Me Unique

As a TEFL-certified tutor, I bring a solid foundation in ESL teaching methodology. I’m also passionate about continuous learning. I stay updated on the latest teaching techniques and language trends. My background in psychology enables me to design effective lessons for all students.

Personal Touch

When I'm not teaching, you can find me exploring new coding projects or reading. These passions fuel my creativity and inform my approach to making lessons engaging and enjoyable.
