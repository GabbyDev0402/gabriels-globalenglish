Personalized ESL Tutoring for Teens & Young Adults

I'm Gabriel, a TEFL-certified ESL tutor passionate about helping teenagers and young adults (12-22) achieve their English language goals. My personalized approach focuses on building a strong foundation in grammar, vocabulary, and conversational English, leading to improved confidence and fluency.

Services Offered

  • Grammar Mastery

    Struggling with English grammar? I'll help you master the rules and apply them correctly through interactive exercises, clear explanations, and personalized feedback.

  • Vocabulary Expansion

    Expand your vocabulary and learn how to use new words effectively in different contexts. We'll use engaging activities and real-world examples to make learning fun and memorable.

  • Conversational Fluency

    Gain confidence in speaking English! We'll practice everyday conversations, focusing on pronunciation, fluency, and natural expression. I'll provide personalized feedback and guidance to help you communicate effectively.

  • Reading Comprehension

    Improve your reading skills and understand complex texts with greater ease. We'll work on strategies for active reading, vocabulary building, and critical analysis.

My Teaching Approach

My teaching style is personalized, engaging, and results-oriented. I create a supportive learning environment where you feel comfortable making mistakes and asking questions. I use a variety of interactive activities, multimedia resources, and clear explanations to make learning fun and effective. We'll work at your own pace, focusing on your individual strengths and weaknesses.

How it Works

  1. Book a Trial Lesson: Schedule a free trial lesson so we can discuss your goals and learning style. Book now.
  2. Personalized Learning Plan: I'll create a customized learning plan tailored to your specific needs.
  3. Engaging Online Lessons: We'll meet online for interactive and effective lessons.
  4. Regular Feedback and Progress Tracking: You'll receive regular feedback to help you track your progress and stay motivated.
